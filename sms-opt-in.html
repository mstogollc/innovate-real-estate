SMS Program Details

Program name: Innovate Real Estate SMS Updates

What you will receive

If you opt in, you will receive recurring text messages from Innovate Real Estate that may include:

  • Appointment reminders and confirmations for tax strategy calls and engineering walkthroughs
  • Follow-ups on your requested Tax Savings Blueprint or Tax Savings Proposal
  • Webinar and educational event invitations
  • Service updates and newsletters about cost segregation, R&D credits, 179D, 263A UNICAP, and other advanced tax strategies
  • Review requests after a completed engagement

Sample messages

"Hi {Name}, this is Lauren with Innovate Real Estate. Thanks for requesting your Tax Savings Blueprint — would you like to schedule a 15-minute strategy call this week? Reply STOP to opt out."
"Reminder: Your cost segregation engineering walkthrough is scheduled for Tuesday at 10am CT. Reply Y to confirm. Reply STOP to opt out."
"New webinar — Dental Industry Tax Strategies on May 13. Save your seat: innovaterealestate.net/events. Reply STOP to opt out."

Frequency & cost

Message frequency varies, typically 2–6 messages per month. Message and data rates may apply based on your wireless carrier plan.

How to opt out

Reply STOP to any message at any time to immediately unsubscribe. You will receive one final confirmation that you have been removed, after which no further messages will be sent.

Privacy & data sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing with subcontractors that support our services (e.g., our SMS platform provider HighLevel/LeadConnector and Twilio) is permitted only as necessary to deliver these messages. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties for any other purpose. See our full Privacy Policy.

Eligibility

U.S. residents only. You must be at least 18 years old and the owner or authorized user of the wireless device. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Other ways to opt in

In addition to this dedicated opt-in form, you may also consent to receive SMS messages from Innovate Real Estate by:

  • Submitting the Tax Savings Blueprint form on our homepage with the SMS consent checkbox checked
  • Submitting the Tax Savings Proposal form on our /submitform page with the SMS consent checkbox checked
  • Verbally consenting and providing your phone number to a representative during an in-person meeting at an industry event we attend or sponsor

In every case, your phone number will only be enrolled if you take an explicit affirmative opt-in action (such as checking a box). We will never enroll a phone number without that consent.
