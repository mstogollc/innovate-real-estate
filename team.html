Rob Schell is a Tax & Financial Advisor and the co-owner of Innovate Real Estate, where he delivers advanced tax strategies and wealth management solutions to growth-focused businesses. A 3-time INC 500 recipient, Rob has become one of the most respected voices in the cost segregation industry.

His philosophy is simple: property owners deserve to keep more of what they earn, and the tax code — when properly understood and applied — is the most powerful tool available to make that happen.

Lauren Burgess

Finance & Economics Expert | Strategic Depreciation Specialist

As a principal of Innovate Real Estate, Lauren helps commercial real estate owners and service industry leaders transform tax strategy into a powerful wealth-building tool. Her journey began in mortgage lending, where she watched entrepreneurs build successful businesses — yet struggle to retain their income at tax time.

After earning her degree in Finance and Economics from the University of South Alabama, she found the solution hiding in plain sight: the IRS tax code itself. Lauren discovered that most property owners are dramatically overpaying their taxes simply because they don't know the rules. Her mission became teaching them to play by those rules — strategically and powerfully.

Through strategic depreciation planning, cost segregation, and advanced methodologies like 263A UNICAP exemptions and tangible property regulations, Lauren has helped clients save millions of dollars — often millions they had no idea were available to them. Her ability to explain complex tax concepts in plain English has made her a sought-after advisor and speaker.
